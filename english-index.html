ABOUT THE COURSE:

The course of MTAM belongs to the undergraduation course of ‘Línguas Estrangeiras Aplicadas ao Multilinguismo e à Sociedade da Informação’ (LEA-MSI) or ‘Foreign Languages Applied to the Multilingualism and the Information Society’.

In its syllabus the topics that will be approached in the course are specified:

  • Knowledge fields related to the course LEA-MSI.
  • Epistemological and methodological questions about the research field in multilingualism.
  • Planning and execution of a research project.
  • Web usability and accessibility.
  • Creating multilingual and accessible websites.

    • The course goals are also specified in the syllabus: The course aims at introducing the methods and techniques applied to multilingualism, presenting some possible forms of developing researches related to one of the base areas of the LEA-MSI course and exploring the usage of informational and technological resources that enable professional experience around digital multilingual areas.

    ABOUT THE WEBSITE:

    In the year of 2020/1, LEA-MSI students had to face the challenge of learn HTML and CSS in a pandemic. The course was reorganized and the students had to present seminars and create a website using HTML. This webpage has the final goal of becoming a memorial oof everything that happened in this course.

  • In the PROJECTS tab you will find all the work that students produced to the course’s seminar. You can download to never forget.
  • In the CONTACT tab you will be able to read more information about LEA-MSI, about professor Cláudio Sampaio Menezes, who taught the course, and the students Pedro Henrique Barros Silvério and Giovana Lacerda Soares, who idealized this website.
